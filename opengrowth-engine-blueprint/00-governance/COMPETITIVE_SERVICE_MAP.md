# Competitive Service Map

This document summarizes the service categories offered by top AI SEO, GEO/AEO, content intelligence, and marketing platforms. It exists to keep OpenGrowth grounded in the real market before development starts.

## Market Reality

The best products are converging around seven service layers:

1. AI visibility monitoring
2. Citation and source intelligence
3. Traditional SEO intelligence
4. Technical and AI crawler accessibility audits
5. Content inventory and optimization
6. Topic, prompt, and content planning
7. Marketing execution and measurement

OpenGrowth should not copy these products feature-for-feature. It should build an evidence-first decision engine that turns these signals into next-best actions and execution.

## Services OpenGrowth Must Eventually Provide

### 1. AI Visibility Intelligence

- Prompt family discovery
- Prompt demand estimation
- Custom prompt tracking
- Platform coverage: ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Google AI Mode, Gemini, Perplexity, Copilot, Claude where available
- Brand mentions
- Competitor mentions
- Sentiment
- Mention prominence
- Answer variance
- Sample size
- Geography and persona variants
- Historical trends

### 2. Citation and Source Intelligence

- Cited URL extraction
- Domain aggregation
- First-party citations
- Competitor citations
- Third-party source opportunities
- Source type classification
- Found-but-not-cited support where providers expose it
- Cited-page analysis
- Citation stability
- Citation gap recommendations

### 3. Prompt Demand and Research

- Search-backed prompt generation
- People Also Ask-style expansion
- Customer-language prompt mining
- Semantic fanout
- Prompt clustering
- Buying-stage mapping
- Persona mapping
- Local prompt variants
- Comparison and alternative prompts
- Prompt volume or demand proxy

### 4. Technical SEO and AI Crawler Access

- Crawlability
- Indexability
- Robots and sitemap
- Canonicals
- Status codes
- Redirect chains
- Metadata
- Structured data
- Internal links
- Mobile/accessibility basics
- AI bot accessibility checks
- Bot log/agent analytics where available

### 5. Content Inventory and Optimization

- GSC-backed content inventory
- Page performance
- Target query mapping
- Content decay detection
- Content grade or quality dimensions
- Topic coverage
- Entity coverage
- Proof and trust signals
- Internal link opportunities
- Refresh recommendations
- Annotations
- Algorithm and LLM update notes

### 6. Topic and Content Planning

- Topic clusters
- Personalized opportunity scoring
- Domain authority/content authority awareness
- SERP and competitor coverage
- Content briefs by type: article, service page, local page, comparison, FAQ, how-to, guide
- Point of view
- Expertise and proof requirements
- Internal/external linking plan
- Measurement plan

### 7. Execution and Measurement

- Generated metadata
- Generated briefs
- Generated drafts
- Claim verification
- Versioning
- Approval workflow
- Export/publishing adapter
- Campaign planning
- Outcome snapshots
- Before/after comparison
- Attribution caveats
- Learning records

## OpenGrowth Differentiation

OpenGrowth should compete by being more transparent and action-focused:

- Every metric has provenance.
- Every recommendation has evidence.
- Every AI visibility result shows sample size and uncertainty.
- Every generated asset shows claims requiring verification.
- Every recommendation has a measurement plan.
- Every completed action feeds a learning loop.
