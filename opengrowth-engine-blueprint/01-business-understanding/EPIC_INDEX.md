# Business Understanding Engine

## Mission

Create a structured, editable model of what the business sells, who it serves, where it competes, why it is credible, and what outcomes it wants.

## Epics

| Epic ID | Epic | Outcome |
|---|---|---|
| BIZ-001 | Business Profile Foundation | Store normalized business identity, market, goals, and voice |
| BIZ-002 | Service and Product Catalogue | Represent services/products with value, audience, geography, and priority |
| BIZ-003 | Audience Segment Model | Define buyer groups, pain points, intent, and conversion fit |
| BIZ-004 | Geographic Market Model | Capture country, region, city, service-area, and local SEO context |
| BIZ-005 | Trust Signal Inventory | Extract and store credentials, testimonials, case studies, guarantees, and proof |
| BIZ-006 | Differentiator Analysis | Identify defensible positioning and weak claims requiring validation |
| BIZ-007 | Website-Informed Business Inference | Infer business facts from crawled pages while labeling them as AI inference |
| BIZ-008 | Assumption Review Workflow | Let users confirm, reject, or edit inferred business facts |
| BIZ-009 | Entity Graph Foundation | Connect services, audiences, places, competitors, pages, and topics |
| BIZ-010 | Business Priority Weighting | Let users weight high-value services and goals for recommendation scoring |
| BIZ-011 | Brand Voice Rules | Store tone, vocabulary, exclusions, compliance notes, and examples |
| BIZ-012 | Business Context API | Provide a stable read model consumed by other engines |

## First Vertical Slice

Start with the smallest slice that creates evidence, produces a decision, and gives the user a meaningful next action.
