# Product vision

## Promise

World-class SEO and marketing intelligence, open to every business.

OpenGrowth exists to answer five practical questions: what should I do next, why does it matter, what could it change, can the work be prepared for me, and did it work?

## Product principles

Every feature must save time, improve a commercial outcome, simplify a decision, produce usable work, or measure a change. Rankings and technical data are inputs, not the product. Estimates stay estimates. Work is never published without review.

## Primary user

The first user is a time-constrained founder, freelancer, small business or agency strategist who needs leverage more than another specialist dashboard. Expert depth remains available through progressive disclosure.

## MVP success

A user can move from a website URL to one high-confidence completed improvement without understanding SEO terminology. The demo optimizes for that moment, not surface-area parity with incumbent suites.
